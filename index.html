Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

About This Application

RMPropWorks Automation is a personal tool used to manage and schedule Pinterest pins for the RMPropWorks Etsy shop (https://rmpropworks.etsy.com). This application is not intended for public use.

Information We Collect

This application only accesses:

  • Our own Pinterest business account data (boards, pins)
  • Our own Etsy product catalog information

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from third parties or other Pinterest users.

How We Use Information

The application uses Pinterest's API solely to:

  • Create and schedule pins featuring our own products
  • Manage our Pinterest boards
  • Track performance of our own pins

Data Storage

All data is stored securely on our private servers. API credentials are encrypted and never shared.

Third-Party Services

This application integrates with:

  • Pinterest API - for pin creation and management
  • Etsy - as the source of our product catalog

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our Etsy shop: rmpropworks.etsy.com
